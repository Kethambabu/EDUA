Layout Regularization Scheme (LRS)

About LRS

The Layout Regularization Scheme (LRS) is a one-time opportunity for property owners to regularize their unauthorized plots/layouts by paying the prescribed charges. This scheme aims to bring unauthorized layouts into the planning framework of EUDA.

Important Notice

Applications for LRS are currently being accepted. Last date for submission: December 31, 2024
