ఏలూరు పట్టణాభివృద్థి సంస్థ (యుడా, EUDA)

The Eluru Urban Development Authority (EUDA) is an urban planning agency in West Godavari district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It was constituted on 1 January 2019, under Andhra Pradesh Metropolitan Region and Urban Development Authority Act, 2016 with the headquarters located at Eluru. The jurisdictional area of EUDA is spread over an area of 3,327.99 km2 (1,284.94 sq mi).It covers 463 villages in 35 mandals of West Godavari district. The below table lists the urban areas of EUDA.

Eluru urban agglomeration is spread over an area of 23.85 km2 (9.21 sq mi) and its constituents include Eluru municipal corporation, census towns of Sanivarapupeta, Satrampadu, Gavaravaram, Tangellamudi, and partly out growths of Komadavole, Eluru (rural) villages.

Mainly a manufacturing city, Eluru produces textiles and leather products but is most noted for its pile carpets. It is also a centre for trade in rice, oilseeds, tobacco, and sugar. The city has a college affiliated with Andhra University in Vizianagaram

The gross district domestic product (GDDP) is ₹45,963 crore (US$6.9 billion) and it contributes 8.8% to the gross state domestic product (GSDP).

There are total 2 Municipality in Eluru.
